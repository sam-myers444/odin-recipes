Cashew Cream

Home

Description

This cashew cream is a perfect base cream to add to nachos, tacos or anyplace you might use sour cream. It can also be adapted into a vegan queso by adding chili pepper paste.

Ingredients

    1 cup raw cashews*
    2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil (or any neutral oil)
    2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
    1 garlic clove
    1/2 tsp salt
    2 tbsp nutritional yeast

Steps

  1. Boil water and combine it with cashews in a blender.
  2. Let soak for 2 minutes.
  3. Add all other ingredients and blend on high speed for 1 minute.
  4. Allow to cool completely and refridgerate overnight.
