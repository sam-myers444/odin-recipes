Super Muffins

Home

Description

These super muffins are delightfully nutty, high in protein, subtly sweet and the perfect companion to a cup of hot coffee.

Ingredients

    2 cups almond flour
    1/2 tsp baking soda
    1/4 tsp sea salt
    1/4 cup of melted oil or butter
    1/4 cup maple syrup
    1 tsp vanilla extract
    1 tsp apple cider vinager
    1/2 cup blueberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Combine almond flour, baking soda and salt.
  3. Mix oil into maple syrup, vinager and vanilla.
  4. Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients until a batter forms.
  5. Add frozen blueberries and scoop muffins batter into a tin.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes turning muffin tin halfway through baking.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
